Lello Learners by Age Group

Each Lello Box is thoughtfully designed to match your child's developmental stage, ensuring activities and prompts are perfectly suited to their reading level, writing abilities, and creative interests.

Ages 4-5: The Discoverers

(Pre-K & Kindergarten)

At this stage, Lello Learners are just starting their journey into letters and words. The focus is on pre-literacy skills and fine motor control.

Reading Level

Pre-reader. They are learning letter sounds and recognizing their name and simple words.

Capabilities

  • Tracing and Drawing: They love to use crayons and markers, learning to stay within the lines.
  • Imaginative Play: They are full of big ideas and love to create stories.
  • Verbal Sharing: They are great at telling you what they think, even if they can't write it all down yet.

Ages 6-8: The Explorers

(1st-2nd Grade)

These learners are starting to read and write on their own. They are gaining confidence and fluency with every word.

Reading Level

Early reader. They are learning to sound out words, recognize sight words, and read simple sentences.

Capabilities

  • Practicing Writing: They are ready to write simple sentences and practice their handwriting.
  • Expressing Ideas: They can start putting their thoughts into simple written form.
  • Engaging with Stories: They love to talk about characters and the plot of a story.

Ages 9-11: The Storytellers

(3rd-5th Grade)

By this age, Lello Learners are fluent readers and writers. They are ready to explore more complex ideas and express their unique voice.

Reading Level

Fluent reader. They can read longer texts with good comprehension and are ready for more advanced vocabulary.

Capabilities

  • Personal Expression: They can write about their feelings, opinions, and observations.
  • Creative Thinking: They are ready for more complex prompts that require planning and thinking about the future.
  • Independent Work: They can follow multi-step instructions and complete projects on their own.

Each Lello Box is carefully crafted to meet your child exactly where they are in their learning journey, providing just the right balance of challenge and support to help them grow and thrive.
